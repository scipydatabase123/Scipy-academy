FAQ

  1. 1. What is SciPy Academy ?
    SciPy Academy is the best IT training and internship institute based in Trivandrum, Kerala, offering industry-relevant courses in software development, AI, data science, full-stack development, and more.
  2. 2.Where is SciPy Academy located ?
    We are located in Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum), Kerala, India. We also offer online training options for learners across India and abroad.
  3. 3.What makes SciPy Academy the best training institute in Kerala ?
    SciPy Academy combines industry-expert faculty, real-time projects, certified internships, and strong placement support, making it a top choice among tech aspirants in Kerala.
  4. 4.What courses are offered at SciPy Academy?
    We offer courses in Python, Full Stack Development (MERN), Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Web Development, Django, Flask, and more.
  5. 5.Are your courses beginner-friendly?
    Yes. We offer beginner to advanced level courses, and no prior coding experience is needed for most beginner programs.
  6. 6.Do you provide course certifications?
    Yes, we provide course completion certificates and internship certificates that are industry-recognized and help enhance your resume.
  7. 7.Can I join SciPy Academy if I'm not from Kerala?
    Absolutely. We offer online courses and internships that can be accessed from anywhere in India or globally.
  8. 8.Does SciPy Academy offer internships?
    Yes. We offer both online and offline internships in Python, AI, Data Science, Web Development, and other trending technologies with real-time project experience.
  9. 9.Are internships at SciPy Academy certified ? Yes, every internship comes with an official SciPy Academy Internship Certificate, which is valuable for college credits,job applications and it is NORKA approved.Their courses and syllabus curriculum follows best practices inspired by the development and community guidelines of NumFOCUS-affiliated scientific computing projects.The syllabus incorporates materials and project-based learning using tools from the NumFOCUS ecosystem, helping students engage with real-world, community-supported software.
  10. 10.How can I apply for an internship at SciPy Academy?
    You can apply directly through our website or by visiting our center in Trivandrum. We also accept college-recommended students for final year projects.
  11. 11.Are your internships suitable for final year students?
    Yes. Our internship programs are ideal for final year students who want real-world experience and project exposure.
  12. 12.Do you provide internship support for BCA, MCA, BTech, or MTech students
    Yes, we have specialized internship programs tailored for BCA, MCA, BSc CS, BTech, and MTech students from various universities.
  13. 13.Who are the trainers at SciPy Academy?
    Our trainers are industry professionals with years of experience in client projects and startups, bringing real-world insights into the classroom.
  14. 14.Do you offer online classes and internships?
    Yes, we offer both live online and offline training, allowing you to choose based on your convenience.
  15. 15.Will I get project work as part of the course?Yes. Every course includes hands-on projects to apply what you’ve learned and build your portfolio.
  16. 16.Do you provide job placement support?
    Yes. SciPy Academy offers resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, and job referrals to companies hiring tech talent.
  17. 17.Can SciPy Academy help me get an internship in a company?
    We offer both in-house internships and assist in referring you to companies based on performance and availability.
  18. 18.Are your certifications helpful for job applications abroad or in India?
    Yes. Our certificates are recognized and accepted by companies across India and can add value when applying for roles internationally.
  19. 19.Which is the best software training institute in Trivandrum?
    SciPy Academy is among the top-rated software training institutes in Trivandrum, known for hands-on training, certified internships, and career-focused programs.
  20. 20.Are you approved for offering internships in Kerala colleges?
    Yes, we partner with various colleges in Kerala and provide MoU-based internship and industrial training programs aligned with university guidelines.
  21. 21.What is the fee structure for your courses and internships?
    Fees vary based on course type and duration. We offer affordable packages, and installment options are available.
  22. 22. How do I enroll at SciPy Academy?
    You can enroll online via our website or by visiting our campus in Trivandrum. Admission is open year-round for most programs.
  23. 23. Do you offer free demo sessions?
    Yes. Contact us to book a free demo class and experience our teaching before enrolling.
  24. 24.What is SciPy?
    SciPy is a powerful open-source Python library used for scientific and technical computing. It is widely used in data science, machine learning, mathematics, and engineering fields.
    At SciPy Academy, we teach Python and the SciPy ecosystem to help students build real-world skills in scientific and data computing.
  25. 25.Is SciPy Academy related to the SciPy Python library?
    No, SciPy Academy is an independent training institute. While we train students in Python and the SciPy library, we are not officially affiliated with the developers of the open-source SciPy library.
  26. 26.Why is your institute named “SciPy Academy”?
    We chose the name "SciPy Academy" to reflect our strong focus on Python, scientific computing, and tech education. Our goal is to empower students with practical knowledge in Python, AI, data science, and full-stack technologies.
  27. 27.Do you teach the SciPy library as part of your curriculum?
    Yes. In our Python and data science courses, we cover the SciPy library, along with NumPy, pandas, Matplotlib, and other core tools used in scientific and machine learning applications.
